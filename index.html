About Us

Who are in extremely love with eco friendly system.

The journey began in 1980 with the launch of a single store for retail business called the New World Company. A few years later, in 1987, the Ansar Group was officially formed with the establishment of an enticing new shopping hub known as the New World Centre.


In 2017 Ansar Group has moved through greater stages of growth from its inception to expansion with 13 massive malls and Real estate properties that have been attracting a steadily rising number of visitors over the years. Our retail presence now extends across three GCC countries and United States of America, which are individually tailored to meet the specific needs and requirements of each market.
